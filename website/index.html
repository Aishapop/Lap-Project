Our Project

Welcome to the portfolio of Easy Online Banking. We are Aisha Kafita and Alan Lin from Thomas More.We created EOB. Our goal was to develop a user-friendly bank application that is simple and easy to use, especially for older people. We named our bank Easy Online Banking because we want to make banking easy and accessible to everyone. We worked tirelessly to ensure that the application is straightforward, with a simple and intuitive interface. With Easy Online Banking, you can perform all your banking activities from the comfort of your home.
